About Me

Currently enrolled full time in BA Graphic Design and Media Arts w/concentration in Web Design ++ (BA.GDM.WDE) program, while continuing to work full time as a Software Engineer. I have been passionate about Art and Technology most of my life.

Fine Art

As a toddler, I used to litter the floor with crumpled papers while drawing endlessly for hours, discarding my imperfect attempts in frustration. As you can see here...I'm still at it. I have a fascination with faces, and the style and techniques of the great 15th & 16th Century Masters.

Web Development

My lifelong love of art and background in commercial art, naturally progressed into web development once this technology became available. I went back to school in 2014 to begin my career in application and web development. It is a constant learning process. It is the learning that keeps me interested in this ever growing and changing field.

Graphic Design

I completed a Commercial Art and Advertising vocational training program my last two years of high school. I learned about paste up back in the day when we were still using paste, and layers meant turning the page on your pad of layout paper. Print media is still being used in some areas, and I have enjoyed relearning this process using todays innovative technologies.
